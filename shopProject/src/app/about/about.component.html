About

This website was founded in 2020 by Esteban Zaragoza, with the goal to sell top quality gaming products available in one spot. He built it from the ground up with the thought of helping others in mind. He personally selects these products and puts them onto the site until a new or better product is released, or just until he finds something better. These products range from the controllers to keyboards, mice and computers. From low to high prices, these products were not just meant for the higher class, but for all classes interested in the immersive world of gaming. Not only can some of these products help you with gaming, but things outside of gaming as well.
